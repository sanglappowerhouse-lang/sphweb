## 🔐 SEPARATION FROM STANDARD MEMBERSHIP

The Dronacharya section is completely separate because:

1. **Different pricing model**: ₹5,000/session (vs ₹600/month membership)
2. **Different CTA**: "Book Owner's Training Program" (vs "Get Started" for membership)
3. **Different value prop**: 1-on-1 elite coaching with founder (vs standard gym access)
4. **Different audience**: Serious athletes/transformation seekers (vs general members)
5. **Different booking system**: Custom form (vs generic contact form)

This ensures premium positioning and prevents confusion with standard offerings.
